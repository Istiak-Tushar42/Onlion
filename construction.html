What We Do

Onlion Construction is a Design/Build General Contractor that specializes in all kinds of renovation projects. We are mostly known as brickwork and brownstone specialist. Our renovation projects vary but primarily are residential and commercial. Whether you have a small or large project, we value each client the same. Preserving the esthetics of our community within Dhaka, Chittagong, Khulna, Barishal, Rajshahi, Sylhet, Rangpur and Mymensingh and surrounding areas, we strive to enhance the construction industry through our continuous break-through and research. At Onlion Construction Co. you will see craftsmanship, dedication, and technology that have changed the face of construction. ONLION CONSTRUCTION is a brownstone & limestone restoration contractor licensed by the city of Dhaka, and is insured and bonded. Our carpenters have all of the experience and expertise you could hope for, but are far more than technically proficient: our carpenters are true craftsmen, who bring a well-honed design sensibility to each and every project, regardless of scope. We are qualified to work on all phases of roofing from repairs to complete roofing replacements. The warranty we offer on our labor is against any malfunctions, including leaks and blow-offs on any roofing repairs and we also offer general contracting, gutters, painting, siding and windows. We guarantee fair and low pricing for our quality work. As experienced general contractors, we are also known for our quick start to completion times. The owner is at every job site. We will handle the entire project ourselves with no sub-contractors, so the project will be done in half the time and at a lower cost.
